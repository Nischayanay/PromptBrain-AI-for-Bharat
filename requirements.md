# PromptBrain - AI-Powered Context Intelligence for Developers

## 1. The Problem: Context Fragmentation Crisis

### Why Indian Developers Struggle
Every day, millions of Indian students and developers face the same frustrating reality:

**The Scattered Workspace Problem:**
- Design mockups in Figma
- Project requirements in Notion  
- Learning conversations in ChatGPT
- Code in VS Code or Cursor
- Documentation across multiple wikis

**The Result?** 
- 6+ hours per week lost to context switching
- Repeated mistakes due to forgotten decisions
- Slower learning curves for students
- Generic AI suggestions that miss project context
- Knowledge silos in development teams

### The AI Assistant Gap
Current AI coding tools (GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, Cursor) are powerful but blind:
- They can't see your Figma designs while you code UI
- They don't know your team's Notion documentation
- They forget yesterday's architectural decisions
- They can't connect concepts across your learning journey

**For Indian Students:** This means AI tutors that can't remember what you learned last week or connect concepts across subjects.

**For Developers:** This means AI assistants that give generic answers instead of project-specific guidance.

## 2. Our Vision: Context-Aware AI for Bharat

**PromptBrain** is an intelligent context layer that makes AI assistants truly understand your work.

### The Big Idea
What if your AI assistant could:
- Remember every design decision you've made
- Access your Figma designs when you're coding UI
- Reference your Notion docs when implementing features
- Connect concepts across your entire learning journey
- Build a living memory of your project's evolution

### How It Works (Simple View)
1. **Connect** your tools (Notion, Figma, ChatGPT, IDE)
2. **Ask** your AI assistant anything, as usual
